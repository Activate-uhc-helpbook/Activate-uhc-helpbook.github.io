Check UHC Card Activation Status

After activating your UHC card, it is important to confirm that activation was successful before using benefits.

How to Check Activation Status

  1. Complete activation on activate.uhc.com
  2. Look for confirmation message
  3. Check UHC member dashboard
  4. Wait 24 hours if status is pending

What If Status Is Not Updated?

  • Refresh page after some time
  • Clear browser cache
  • Contact UHC customer support
