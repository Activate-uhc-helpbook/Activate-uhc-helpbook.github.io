Activate UHC Card on Mobile (Android & iPhone)

UnitedHealthcare members can easily activate their UHC card on mobile devices. The activate.uhc.com website is fully optimized for smartphones and tablets, allowing secure activation without a computer.

Steps to Activate UHC Card on Mobile

  1. Open Chrome, Safari, or Edge on your mobile device
  2. Visit the official website: activate.uhc.com
  3. Enter your UHC member ID and card information
  4. Complete identity verification
  5. Confirm activation and wait for success message

Mobile Activation Tips

  • Disable VPN or private browsing
  • Use updated browser versions
  • Ensure stable internet connection
  • Allow cookies and JavaScript
